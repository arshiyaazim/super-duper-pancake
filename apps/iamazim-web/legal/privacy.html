Privacy Policy

Al-Aqsa Security & Logistics Services Ltd.  |  Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Al-Aqsa Security & Logistics Services Ltd. ("we", "our", or "the Company") is committed to protecting the privacy of our clients, employees, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at iamazim.com or contact us for services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information: name, phone number, email address, and company name when you submit a quote request or contact form.
  • Service information: details about the security services you inquire about, location of service, and specific requirements.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and pages visited — collected automatically via server logs for security and performance purposes.
  • Employee data: for hired personnel — identification documents, training records, attendance, and payroll information required by Bangladesh labour law.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your service inquiries and provide requested security services.
  • To process employment applications and manage employee records.
  • To send service updates, invoices, and operational communications.
  • To comply with legal obligations under Bangladesh law.
  • To improve our website and service offerings.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only:

  • With government authorities when required by law or for licensing compliance.
  • With our service partners (payroll processors, background verification agencies) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • In connection with a business merger, acquisition, or asset transfer — in which case users will be notified.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our digital operations use encrypted communications and access-controlled systems.

6. Data Retention

Client inquiry data is retained for 3 years. Employee records are retained as required by Bangladesh labour regulations. You may request deletion of your personal data at any time, subject to legal retention requirements.
