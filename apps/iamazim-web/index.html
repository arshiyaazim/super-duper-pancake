About Us

A Strategic Partner in Safeguarding Bangladesh

Al-Aqsa Security & Logistics Services Ltd. was founded in 2014 with a singular mission: to provide comprehensive, reliable, and highly professional security solutions across diverse industries in Bangladesh.

We are more than just a security company — we are a strategic partner in safeguarding your assets, ensuring the safety of your personnel, and maintaining the integrity of your operations. From routine patrols to high-risk armed escort services, our versatile team delivers bespoke solutions tailored to each client's unique challenges.

Our founder, Md. Muradul Alam Azim, leveraged five years of international operations management experience at G4S Security in Dubai to establish world-class security standards in Bangladesh — bringing international expertise directly to the local market.
