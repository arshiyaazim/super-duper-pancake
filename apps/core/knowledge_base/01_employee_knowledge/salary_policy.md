# Salary Policy

## Purpose
Provide employee-safe salary, bonus, advance, payment, and deduction rules.

## Scope
This article is visible to employees and candidates where salary information is appropriate.

## Salary Structures
### Escort / Survey Scout
- Training period: 45 days.
- Training pay: ৳10,000-৳15,000/month.
- After training: duty-based payment, normally within ৳12,000-৳18,000 range.

### Security Guard
Probationary monthly package may be up to ৳17,000:
- basic salary: ৳9,000;
- house rent: ৳2,000;
- attendance bonus: ৳2,000;
- monthly evaluation incentive: ৳2,000;
- mobile bill: ৳300;
- transport: ৳700;
- provident fund: ৳500;
- insurance: ৳500.

Permanent monthly package may be up to ৳24,700:
- basic salary: ৳12,000;
- house rent: ৳4,000;
- attendance bonus: ৳2,000;
- monthly evaluation incentive: ৳3,000;
- mobile bill: ৳500;
- transport: ৳1,500;
- provident fund: ৳1,200;
- insurance: ৳500.

## Salary Payment Date
Normal salary payment date is between the 10th and 12th of each month.

If an employee is unauthorized absent between the 1st and 10th of the month, salary may be held and paid between the 20th and 25th.

## Attendance Bonus
- Probation period: ৳2,000/month.
- Permanent employment: ৳2,000/month.
- Full attendance bonus depends on regular attendance.
- Unauthorized absence may cancel or reduce the attendance bonus.

## Advance Payment
Employees may request an advance for urgent needs.

Normal guideline:
- ৳500-৳1,000 may be provided depending on situation.
- This is a guideline, not a hard cap.
- Final approval depends on company review and employee duty verification.

Advance amount is adjusted or deducted from final salary/payment.

## Payment Method
Salary and allowance are usually sent to the employee's own bKash or Nagad number.

Employees must provide the correct payment method and number every time payment is requested.

## Deductions
- 1 day unauthorized absence may cause up to 2 days deduction.
- 3 consecutive late arrivals may cause 1 day salary deduction.
- Advance payments are deducted from final salary or bill.
- Less than 30 working days may result in daily-rate calculation instead of full package.

## Provident Fund
Provident fund may be claimed after 2 years of service.

Monthly provident fund amount:
- probation: ৳500;
- permanent: ৳1,200.

## Client Service Charge Clarification
Client billing/service charge, such as ৳13,000 for guard service, is not employee salary.

## FAQ
**প্রশ্ন: বেতন কবে দেয়?**  
উত্তর: সাধারণত প্রতি মাসের ১০-১২ তারিখের মধ্যে বেতন দেওয়া হয়। অনুমোদনহীন অনুপস্থিতি থাকলে বেতন ২০-২৫ তারিখে যেতে পারে।

**প্রশ্ন: কত অগ্রিম পাব?**  
উত্তর: পরিস্থিতি বিবেচনায় সাধারণত ৳500-৳1,000 পর্যন্ত অগ্রিম পাওয়া যেতে পারে, তবে এটি কঠোর সীমা নয় এবং অনুমোদনের উপর নির্ভর করে।
