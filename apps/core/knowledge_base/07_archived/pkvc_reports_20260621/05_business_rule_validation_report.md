# PKVC Report 05: Business Rule Validation Report

- Date: 2026-06-21
- Scope: Business rule existence, classification, and documentation quality

## Result
- Status: FAILED
- Observation: many rules exist and are enforceable in production, but a significant subset is not fully documented or authority-linked.

## Rule Categories Checked
- Eligibility and role gating
- Financial and payroll formulas
- Complaint and escalation handling
- Auto-reply and safety controls
- Release and attendance derivation rules
- Recruitment constraints and scoring

## Key Gaps
- Hidden operational rules not represented in article form
- Mixed formula interpretations in financial flows
- Insufficient management authority references for disputed rules

## Required Remediation
- Document each production rule with examples and visibility class
- Attach authoritative source and management owner
- Resolve financial rule conflicts before certification

## Business Rule Conclusion
Business rule documentation is not yet complete or conflict-free for PKVC sign-off.
