# Release Slip

## Purpose
Explain what a release slip is and why it is required for escort duty completion and payment.

## Scope
This article is visible to escorts and relevant employees.

## Definition
A release slip is the signed release information written on or attached to an escort's duty slip after vessel work is completed.

It usually includes:
- release date;
- release time;
- supervisor or ghat in-charge signature;
- duty/vessel context.

## Workflow
1. Escort completes vessel duty.
2. Ghat in-charge or supervisor writes release date and time and signs the duty slip.
3. Escort takes a clear photo of the release slip.
4. Escort sends the photo to the official WhatsApp channel.
5. Company reviews the release slip.
6. Final duty days, food money, conveyance, advance adjustment, and payment are calculated.

## Business Rules
- Release slip is required to finalize escort duty days.
- Release slip is required before final escort payment.
- The slip photo must be clear and readable.
- Missing or unclear signature/date/time may delay payment.

## Examples
If an escort finishes duty at a destination point, the supervisor writes the release date and time and signs the duty slip. That signed document becomes the release slip.
