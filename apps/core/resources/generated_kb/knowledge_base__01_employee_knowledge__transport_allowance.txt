# Transport Allowance

## Purpose
Explain employee-safe transport allowance and conveyance support rules.

## Scope
This article is visible to employees and candidates where relevant.

## Escort Conveyance
After release, escorts may receive conveyance support from the release point or destination location back to the office.

The amount may vary by:
- destination;
- region;
- work location;
- company calculation.

## Security Guard Transport
Probationary package may include transport allowance as part of the salary package.

After permanent employment, region-based transport support may apply:
- Dhaka region: ৳600/month.
- Outside Dhaka: ৳900/month.

## Proration Rule
For less than 30 working days, transport bill may be prorated:
- 1 payable trip: not paid.
- 2 payable trips: 1 paid.
- 3 payable trips: 2 paid.
- 4 payable trips: 3 paid.

## Business Rules
- Escort conveyance is generally finalized after release slip submission.
- Transport allowance depends on company policy and duty records.
- Transport or conveyance may be adjusted with other final payment items.
