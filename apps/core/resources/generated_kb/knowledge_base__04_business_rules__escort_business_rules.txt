# Escort Business Rules

## Source Of Truth
- Escort training is 45 days and paid ৳10,000-৳15,000/month.
- After training, escort payment is duty-based, normally ৳12,000-৳18,000 range.
- Escort duty day is 24 hours, day plus night.
- Escort final calculation requires release slip.

## Order Rules
- Client order creates AI draft, not final confirmation.
- Admin must review escort name, mobile, start date, and shift before client confirmation.
- Roster update occurs only after final client confirmation.

## Release Rules
- Release slip must include release date/time and supervisor/ghat signature.
- Missing/unclear slip routes to manual review.

## Transport Allowance Rules

### Purpose
Define the hardcoded transport allowance rates applied to every escort release payment calculation.

### Transport Rate Table (Production Authoritative)

| Destination | Rate (BDT) |
|---|---|
| Dhaka / Narayanganj | ৳600 |
| Faridpur | ৳700 |
| Mongla | ৳800 |
| Barishal / Coastal | ৳900 |
| Khulna / Jessore | ৳1,000 |
| Default (any other) | ৳600 |

**Business Rule:** Transport allowance is paid to the Master/Sukani, not the escort. It is calculated per program based on the release point destination.

**Validation:** The release point field in the release slip is matched to the destination table. Unrecognized destinations receive the default ৳600 rate.

**Exception:** Transport rates are hardcoded in `escort_lifecycle._TRANSPORT_RATES`. They are NOT synchronized to the `escort_calculation_config` database table. The release slip draft shows an estimate. Changes to rates require a developer code update.

**Management Authority:** Mongla rate of ৳800 approved by management (CON-03, PKVC Decision HK-19).

**Source Module:** `modules/escort_lifecycle`
**Source Function:** `_TRANSPORT_RATES`
**PKCA Report:** 10_hidden_rule_coverage_report.md (HK-19)

## Food Cost Rules

### Purpose
Define how food costs are calculated and recorded for escort programs.

### Calculation Rules

**Base rate:** ৳150 BDT per duty day

**Payment recipient:** Master/Sukani (not the escort)

**Time exceptions (when a duty day's food is excluded):**

| Condition | Food Excluded |
|---|---|
| Release slip arrives before 10:00 AM | Release-day food is NOT counted |
| Escort boards (starts duty) after 3:00 PM | Boarding-day food is NOT counted |

**Accounting rule:** Food amount paid is recorded as an escort advance and deducted from the escort's payroll.

**Business Rule:** Food cost is always an estimate in the release draft. It uses the hardcoded ৳150/day rate. The release draft is a DRAFT ONLY until admin approves.

**Example:**
- Escort on duty 5 days, boards at 9:00 AM, released at 11:00 AM
- Release-day food included (arrived after 10 AM)
- Food = 5 × ৳150 = ৳750

**Management Authority:** CON-04 approved in PKVC Management Decisions (food=150/day, time exceptions apply).

**Source Module:** `modules/escort_lifecycle`
**Source Function:** `_calc_duty_days()`, `_FOOD_RATE`
**PKCA Report:** 10_hidden_rule_coverage_report.md (HK-20)

## Duty Day Validation

### Suspicious Duty Day Rule

**Business Rule:** If calculated duty days exceed 90, the release slip draft includes a SUSPICIOUS warning for admin review.

**Purpose:** Guards against data entry errors where a program appears to have lasted more than 3 months.

**Validation:** Admin must explicitly review and approve releases with duty_days > 90.

**Source Module:** `modules/escort_lifecycle`
**Source Function:** `build_release_draft()`
**PKCA Report:** 09_state_machine_coverage_report.md (HK-23)

### Release Date Validation

**Business Rule:** Release dates are rejected if:
- The date is in the future (future release not allowed)
- The date is more than 1 year in the past (stale data protection)

**Source Module:** `modules/escort_lifecycle`
**Source Function:** `_validate_release_date()`
**PKCA Report:** 09_state_machine_coverage_report.md (HK-22)

## Payment Formula

**Management Decision (CON-01, CON-02):**

**Authoritative daily rate formula:** `12000 / 30 * duty_days`

- This gives an effective daily rate of ৳400/day
- The formula is used in both the payment workflow and payroll module
- The formula is derived from the ৳12,000 fixed monthly base salary, not a separate fixed daily slab

**Source Module:** `modules/payment_workflow`, `modules/payroll`
**PKCA Report:** 04_business_rule_coverage_report.md
**Management Authority:** CON-01 and CON-02 approved in PKVC Management Decisions
