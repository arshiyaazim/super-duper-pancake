# Joining Business Rules

## Candidate-Facing Source Of Truth
No advance payment is mandatory to get the job. The company can help serious candidates join without advance payment.

## Practical Internal Fee Structure
- Form fee: ৳330, one-time, non-refundable.
- Standard joining fee: ৳3,500.
- Minimum practical joining payment: ৳1,000 + ৳330 = ৳1,330.
- Remaining joining fee can be adjusted from salary.
- Monthly installment: ৳500.
- If employee completes 6 months, ৳3,500 is returned over next 7 months.
- If employee leaves before 6 months, ৳3,500 is not returned.
- Fee is not bribe or deposit; it is related to uniform, ID, and training costs.

## AI Reply Rule
If candidate asks about joining fee, AI must say advance payment is not mandatory and company can help candidates join first, then adjust later if needed.
